About Me

My fascination with computers began the moment I first encountered them as a child. I was captivated by how they worked, and I spent countless hours exploring various software, learning the intricacies of computer systems, and teaching myself how to build websites. This early passion laid the foundation for a lifelong journey of exploration and innovation in the intersection of technology and law.

Despite my deep interest in technology, I chose to pursue my dream of becoming a lawyer, enrolling in a prestigious law school in Shanghai to study international economic law. While I excelled academically, consistently achieving top grades, I never let go of my love for computers. I continued to delve into the world of computer games and self-study various aspects of technology, balancing my legal studies with my passion for tech.

After achieving my goal of becoming a lawyer, I embarked on a new journey to merge my two passions: law and technology. I began exploring how data science could revolutionize legal research, from leveraging computer vision to enhance trademark searches to providing data-driven consultations for clients. I also focused on building and maintaining databases to improve the efficiency of case data retrieval. The rapid advancements in AI opened my eyes to the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in the legal field, particularly in areas such as contract compliance and predictive analytics.

Recognizing the growing importance of data protection, privacy, and information security for international clients, I decided to steer my career toward this critical area. While continuing my legal career, I pursued a master's degree in computer science, further deepening my technical expertise. Now, I am fully committed to mastering information security, currently undertaking a full-time master's program in this field. My ultimate goal is to bridge the gap between law and technology, becoming the most tech-savvy lawyer and the most legally proficient computer engineer.

In my professional life, I am known for being rational, analytical, and collaborative, always striving to find innovative solutions to complex problems. In my personal life, I embrace a go-with-the-flow attitude, valuing quality time and the simple pleasures of life. I find peace in nature and enjoy spending time outdoors whenever possible. As you may have noticed from this website, I have a deep love for dogs and animals in general. Watching animal documentaries is one of my favorite ways to unwind.
